Intro

Hi, I'm a university student currently studying in Kyoto. My work sits at the intersection of the physical and digital, ranging from tactile independent publishing to real-time creative coding projects.

I enjoy experimenting with how we interact with information, whether it's through the fold of a page or a digital interface.

Works

Independent Publishing: ZINE Project

This project explores the structural design of ZINEs, specifically focusing on the accordion (jyabara) fold. By using unique physical formats, I aim to create a more interactive and tactile reading experience.

About

I am an international student currently pursuing my studies in Japan. Living in Kyoto has deeply influenced my creative process, specifically in how I view traditional craftsmanship alongside modern technology.

自己紹介
